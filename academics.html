Academic Policies & Student Support

Registration & Credits

Min 12 credits / Max 21 per semester (undergrad). Priority registration by class standing. Drop with W period ends week 10. Summer session flexible.

Academic Integrity

Plagiarism, cheating, unauthorized collaboration – sanctions from warning to dismissal. Appeal process via Academic Conduct Committee. Honor Code pledge.

Grading & GPA

Letter grades A-F, 4.0 scale. C- minimum for major courses. Grade replacement policy for up to 2 repeated courses. Incomplete contracts available.

Inclusivity & Accessibility

Disability Resource Center: exam accommodations, note-taking, accessible formats. Counseling & Psychological Services (CAPS). Religious holiday accommodation.

Grievance & Appeals

Grade appeals, academic standing, student complaints. Ombudsperson office. Formal path: department chair → Dean → University Academic Senate.

Signature Academic Programs

Honors College

Selective admission (top 10%). Interdisciplinary honors seminars, early research, senior thesis or creative project. Priority registration and dedicated advisors.

Global Learning

Exchange with University of Edinburgh, NUS, Keio, etc. Faculty-led winter/summer programs. Credit transfer pre-approved. Language & culture requirement option.

Co-op & Corporate Partnership

Mandatory for engineering & business: paid co-op semesters. Over 200+ partners (Google, Siemens, Epic). Industry mentors, project-based capstone with real clients.

Summer Session & Pre-College

Intensive 5-week courses, international summer institute, research experience for undergrads (REU). High school scholars program available.
