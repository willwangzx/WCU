Application Process

How the admissions committee evaluates each application.

1. Submit Application

Complete the online form with your academic background, intended program, activities, and contact details.

2. Upload Materials

Provide a personal statement, and one substantial sample of work such as research, code, design, or analytical writing.

3. Committee Review

The admissions committee considers preparation, initiative, communication, and demonstrated readiness for inquiry-driven study.

4. Decision & Enrollment

Admitted students receive an offer packet with scholarship outcome, enrollment deadlines, and next-step guidance.

Required Materials

  • We do NOT need academic transcript; standardized test scores are optional!
  • Personal statement of 500 to 800 words.
  • One project portfolio, writing sample, or research sample.

Important Dates

  1. Priority application review closes June 4th.
  2. Faculty interviews begin after the June 4th deadline.
  3. Decision releases begin after July 8th on a rolling basis.
  4. Enrollment confirmation deadline is August 1st.

International Applicants

  • Visa support begins immediately after enrollment confirmation.
  • Digital interviews are available across time zones.
  • Scholarship and housing guidance are included in the offer packet.

Selection Principles

We look for academic seriousness, originality, and the capacity to grow.

WCU does not evaluate merit through scores alone. Strong applicants show evidence of disciplined study, thoughtful communication, and meaningful effort in one or more areas of work. The committee values applicants whose records suggest future contribution, not merely past compliance.
