Undergraduate Studies

Learning Format

  • Small seminar sections paired with project studios
  • Faculty-led research and design labs from the first year
  • Cross-school electives that support double majors and thematic pathways
  • Portfolio, capstone, and public presentation requirements in many programs

Core Curriculum (Gen Ed)

  • Foundations: Quantitative Reasoning, Writing & Rhetoric
  • Scientific Inquiry with Lab
  • Global & Historical Perspectives
  • Ethics & Civic Engagement
  • Creative Expression

Academic Opportunities

  • Honors Program & Capstone Thesis
  • Study Abroad (35+ exchange partners)
  • Undergraduate Research Fellowship (stipend + faculty mentor)
  • Internship for credit (Co-op eligible)

Student Support

  • Writing Center (peer and professional consultations)

Graduate Studies

Master's & Doctoral Programs

Research-based: M.S./Ph.D. in CS, Engineering, Economics; Professional: MBA, M.Ed., DBA, Ed.D. in Leadership. Also certificate options in Data Science, AI Ethics.

Thesis & non-thesis tracks

Admission Requirements

Minimum GPA 3.0/4.0; English proficiency (TOEFL 90 / IELTS 7.0); 2-3 recommendation letters; research proposal for PhD. Full details →

Funding & Assistantships

Graduate Teaching/Research Assistantships (full tuition + stipend). Merit scholarships, external fellowships. Over 85% of PhD students receive full funding.

Degree Completion

Coursework (30-72 credits), comprehensive exams, dissertation/project defense. Annual progress reviews. PhD timeline: 4-5 years.

Academic Resources

WCU Library System

3 million volumes, 400+ databases, subject librarians. 24/7 access to study commons, group rooms, and digital archives. Borrowing: 6 weeks for books, online renewals.

Learning & Tutoring Centers

Math Emporium, Writing Fellows, Language Corner (ESL support). Peer-led workshops and exam prep sessions. Located in Student Success Hub.

Labs & Makerspaces

Fab Lab (3D printers, CNC), VR Studio, Electronics Prototyping, Wet labs for bioengineering. Reserve equipment online.

Digital Toolkit

Canvas LMS, Adobe Creative Cloud, MATLAB, Python/JupyterHub, university email and OneDrive. Free software downloads for students.

University Bookstore

Textbooks new/used, rental options, digital access codes. Price match and buyback program. Order online for pickup.

Continue Education

Honors College

Selective admission (top 10%). Interdisciplinary honors seminars, early research, senior thesis or creative project. Priority registration and dedicated advisors.

Distinction on diploma

Global Learning

Exchange with University of Edinburgh, NUS, Keio, etc. Faculty-led winter/summer programs. Credit transfer pre-approved. Language and culture requirement option.

Co-op & Corporate Partnership

Mandatory for engineering and business: paid co-op semesters. Over 200+ partners (Google, Siemens, Epic). Industry mentors, project-based capstone with real clients.

Summer Session & Pre-College

Intensive 5-week courses, international summer institute, research experience for undergrads (REU). High school scholars program available.
