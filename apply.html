Before You Start

Prepare these materials

  • We do NOT need academic transcript; standardized test scores are optional!
  • Personal statement describing your goals and interests.
  • One project, portfolio, or writing sample.
  • Passport or legal name details for enrollment records.

Sample Application

Example of a strong submission profile.

Applicant Snapshot

Name: Maya Chen

Intended Program: Computer Science and Mathematics

Current School: Hangzhou International Academy

Portfolio: GitHub project archive, data visualization portfolio, and a machine learning research abstract.

Statement Direction

Maya explains how she became interested in trustworthy AI through a local public-health data project, then connects that experience to her goal of studying computational modeling and socially responsible systems design at WCU.

Additional Context

This optional field can be used to explain unusual grading periods, independent projects, internships, competitions, or personal circumstances that shaped the application.
